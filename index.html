Molka is South Korea's Latest Epidemic

"Molka" is the Korean term for hidden cameras or miniature spy cameras secretly and illegally installed, often in order to capture voyeuristic images and videos.

Molka is an abbreviation of molrae-kamera, which means a sneaky camera.

South Korean women are not safe from this perverted digital crime.
